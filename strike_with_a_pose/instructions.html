• Press T to turn on Translate mode. Click and drag the mouse to move the object in the x and y directions. Scroll the mouse wheel to move the object in the z direction.
• Press R to turn on Rotate mode. Click and drag the mouse to rotate the object.
• Press A to turn on Ambient Light mode. Scroll the mouse wheel to change the ambient light intensity.
• Press D to turn on Directional Light mode. Scroll the mouse wheel to change the directional light intensity. Click and drag the mouse to move the directional light source.
• Press V to turn on Angle of View mode. Scroll the mouse wheel to change the angle of view.
• Press Q to Query the neural network (i.e., generate a prediction).
• Press B to toggle the Background.
• Press G display a link to the project's GitHub repo.
